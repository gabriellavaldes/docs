# Aptible Hosted Pricing

Ideal for startups or organizations that prefer not to manage their own infrastructure, the Aptible-hosted pricing option offers a comprehensive, fully managed solution. This offering includes both the Aptible platform fee and all infrastructure costs, wrapped into a simple, usage-based pricing model. With this option, companies can focus more on their core business activities while Aptible handles infrastructure hosting and management.

# Self Hosted Pricing

<Info> Currently, AWS is the only cloud supported cloud.</Info>


Ideal for organizations that already existing AWS usage or organizations interested in host their own infrastructure, the self-hosted pricing model allows businesses to use the Aptible platform directly within their own AWS accounts. This offering offers increased flexibility, including full access to AWS integrations, the ability to leverage AWS discounts and credits, and direct payment for infrastructure usage to AWS. The platform fee under this model is custom, and interested companies should contact Aptible directly for more details.
